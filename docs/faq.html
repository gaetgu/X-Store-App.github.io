These are the most asked questions here at XStore. Any questions? Visit our Discord

  • Can I publish Windows only apps to XStore?

    Yes, you can do that. But users not from that OS won't be able to download it. See Wine support blog.

  • What targets are available?

    Currently the supported targets are MSIX, MSI, ZIP (outpacked), APK, EXE, APPIMAGE and DEB.

  • What about Apple?

    We sadly can't support Apple devices because they're too closed. This could change in the future.

  • I want to publish a package to XStore. Can I do that?

    You can't do that now. But it's in the To-Do list.
