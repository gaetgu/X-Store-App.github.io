Features

XStore is a 100% free app store for open-source projects developed by the open-source community!
XStore has apps for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. XStore's source is written in HTML
and Javascript with the help of Electron, as well as Dart on top of the Flutter framework. Xstore
has cross-platform sync, which means that if you get an app on your Windows computer,
you can use it on your iMac or even your phone!
